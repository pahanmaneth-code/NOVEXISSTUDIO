Ask Us Anything
How long does it take to create an invitation?
Typically 2–5 working days from our initial discussion to the final launch. Rush delivery is available upon request.
Can guests RSVP through the invitation?
Yes — the Premium package includes a fully functional Smart RSVP system with instant phone notifications and automatic Google Sheet sync.
Can we edit details after it is launched?
Absolutely. Minor updates such as times, venue details, or messages can be made at any point before your wedding day.
Does the countdown timer work correctly?
Yes — the countdown is locked to your exact wedding date and time. It will always display the correct time remaining, regardless of when hosting starts.
Can guests specify how many people are attending?
Yes. Our RSVP forms allow guests to enter their exact pax (guest count), making it incredibly easy to finalise catering and seating arrangements.
Does it work properly on all phones?
Yes — every invitation is built mobile-first and thoroughly tested across all devices and browsers for a flawless experience.
Can we use our own photos and music?
Of course. Your photos and your chosen background music are central to making the invitation authentically yours.
How do guests access the invitation?
A single clean, shareable link is all they need. Send it via WhatsApp, email, or any platform. No app download required.
